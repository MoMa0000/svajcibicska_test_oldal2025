The main things here are the SFI (Solar Flux Index), the K-Index (Marked as "K") and the A-Index (Marked as "A").

SFI - Summarization of the Sun's Radiation Output

  • 70 - Not Good
  • 80 - Good
  • 90 - Better
  • 100+ - Best

A-Index - Daily Average of Magnetic Activity

  • A=0-7 Quiet
  • A=8-15 Unsettled
  • A=16-29 Active
  • A=30-49 Minor storm
  • A=50-99 Major storm
  • A=100-400 Severe storm

K-Index Updated every 3 Hours

  • K=0 Inactive
  • K=1 Very quiet
  • K=2 Quiet
  • K=3 Unsettled
  • K=4 Active
  • K=5 Minor storm
  • K=6 Major storm
  • K=7 Severe storm
  • K=8 Very severe storm
  • K=9 Extremely severe storm
